Why this School

This is Project based learning which offeres students boundless education. Students can explore their interests and focus on their career instead of having to study everything conventionally. Project based learning also helps the students to be more responsible and mature than the rest.

Content vs Skills

In today's technological world, skills are reputated more than content. Studies show that people with the right skills perform better at that task than people with content for the same task. Over here at Rise Up we push students to acquire right skills rather getting content & knowledge to help them in real world.

Amenities

Workshop rooms

We have created 2 workshop rooms wherein we have resources like sawing machine, woods, nails, hammers, etc.

Science labs

We have 2 science labs for, biology, chemistry and physics. Students can come here for experiments or going through diagrams of science.

Playgrounds

We have created a lot of open grounds through rooms where students can play outdoor games and even work outside with the nature.

Open space classrooms

All our classrooms have been made like our MLC space, open spaces where students can work on their projects.

Library

A silent place for students to read and work. We will have a lot of books on different genres as all the students might not have the facility to research online.

Music Rooms

Facilities like guitar, keyboard, drums and mics. We will be providing them with mentors according to the instrument they want to learn.

Multipurpose rooms

A place that can be used for anything and everything!

Amphitheatre

An area where we can have concerts by students, drama, performances. We can also have extra meetings and gatherings there.

Compost & Kitchen Garden

Near the kitchen we have a kitchen garden where we will be growing our own crops and vegetables,we also have a compost machine in a corner where we will get natural fertilizer.
